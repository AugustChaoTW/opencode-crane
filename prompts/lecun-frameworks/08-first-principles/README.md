# First Principles Deconstructer

A framework for stripping away assumptions and rebuilding understanding from the ground up, inspired by Yann LeCun's intellectual tradition of questioning everything.

## Core Concept
The First Principles Deconstructer identifies the fundamental truths of a topic by systematically removing layers of conventional wisdom and unproven assumptions. It forces a logical reconstruction of a field or strategy based only on what's verifiable, revealing hidden opportunities where the status quo is built on shaky foundations.

## Core LeCun Concept
Yann LeCun's career is defined by his refusal to accept conventional wisdom. In the 1990s and early 2000s, the AI community largely abandoned neural networks, labeling them a "dead" technology. LeCun ignored this consensus, sticking to first principles about how visual information should be processed. This persistence led to the invention and refinement of Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs), which eventually powered the modern deep learning revolution. This framework captures that spirit: if the experts are just repeating each other, they're probably missing something fundamental.

## When to Use
- When an industry practice feels "standard" but lacks clear logic.
- Before making a major strategic pivot or investment.
- When you suspect a long-held belief in your field is actually a "repeated tradition" rather than a fact.
- To find contrarian advantages in crowded markets.

## The Prompt
```markdown
You are a researcher trained in Yann LeCun's intellectual tradition of questioning EVERYTHING from first principles — his refusal to accept conventional wisdom just because everyone believes it, which led him to invent CNNs when the entire AI community said neural networks were dead.

I need a first-principles analysis that strips away assumptions and rebuilds understanding from the ground up.

Deconstruct:
- Conventional wisdom inventory: what does everyone in this space believe to be true without questioning it
- Assumption autopsy: for each belief, what evidence actually supports it vs what's just repeated tradition
- First principles isolation: strip away all assumptions and identify the fundamental truths that cannot be argued
- Rebuilding from truth: starting from ONLY these first principles, what conclusions logically follow
- Conventional vs first-principles gap: where does the rebuilt understanding differ from conventional wisdom
- Contrarian opportunities: which conventional beliefs are WRONG, and what advantage does knowing that create
- Historical pattern: has this field had moments before where everyone believed something that turned out to be false
- Expert consensus vs reality: are the experts right because they've thought deeply, or are they just repeating each other
- Implications of being right: if my first-principles conclusion is correct, what should I do differently from everyone else

Format as a first-principles analysis with conventional beliefs listed, each one challenged, and rebuilt conclusions from ground truth.

[YOUR TOPIC: Describe the belief, industry practice, conventional wisdom, or strategy you want deconstructed from first principles]
```

## Output Format
The AI will provide a structured report covering:
1. **Conventional Wisdom Inventory:** A list of "standard" beliefs.
2. **Assumption Autopsy:** A breakdown of the evidence (or lack thereof) for those beliefs.
3. **Fundamental Truths:** The isolated first principles.
4. **The Logical Rebuild:** Conclusions derived solely from the truths.
5. **Gap Analysis:** Where the status quo fails compared to the rebuilt logic.
6. **Actionable Implications:** What you should do differently to exploit the gaps.

## Usage Tips
- **Be Specific:** The more detailed your "topic" description, the better the deconstruction. Instead of "Marketing," use "The belief that B2B marketing requires a 6-month lead nurturing sequence."
- **Iterate on Truths:** If the "First Principles Isolation" section feels too broad, ask the AI to dig deeper into a specific truth it identified.
- **Look for the "Why":** Focus on the "Assumption Autopsy" to understand exactly where the conventional wisdom started to diverge from reality.
